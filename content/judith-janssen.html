Judith Janssen

Ecoidealist & Founding Member
Board Member & Staff

Judith has a masters degree in International law from Tilburg (NL) and Granada (ES) University. Her experience ranges from training and project management in Google to organisational management experience working as part of the executive management team for a non-profit Faculty organisation. Judith is highly interested in social entrepreneurism and developing sustainable and innovative solutions to social and environmental problems and strongly believes in community empowerment as one of the key driving forces for social and thus global change.

Judith brings her skills in organising trainings and in project management, along with her experience working in larger companies. This not only helps to round-out the company's ability to manage itself, but directly relates to our service offerings and revenue generation.
