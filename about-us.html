TEPUI: Sustainability Partnerships with Nature

TEPUI design is a consultation and strategy design company currently working to develop applications of Biochar for restoring damaged soils, locally, regionally and internationally. We also work to promote urban food production and uptake of Living Technologies, including Green Roofs and Living Facades on buildings. Additional research includes trials of wild varieties of economic plants from around the world for productivity in the Irish climate.

Our Challenge: The Environment

TEPUI Design was launched in 2007, to address serious issues such as water quality and availability, food and energy security, loss of biodiversity and build up of greenhouse gase.

Our Way: Open Collaboration

In order to achieve our goal, TEPUI brings bodies from the private, educational and governmental sectors to work together. This collaborative synergy will not only benefit these sectors individually, but also our society and environment as a whole.
